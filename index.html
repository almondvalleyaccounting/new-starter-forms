Proof of Concept

Forms are functional but not yet connected to a backend. Data does not save. For review purposes only.

How it works
1

Employee Fills In Their Details

The employer shares a unique link with their new starter. The employee completes their personal details, bank info, tax status, and student loan information.

Open Employee Form →
2

Employer Completes Onboarding

The employer is notified by email that their new starter has submitted details. They click through to complete pay, working pattern, holiday, and other employment terms.

Open Employer Form →
3

Payroll Team Processes

Both sets of data land in a Google Sheet (one per employer). Your team is notified, exports to CSV, and imports into BrightPay. The record moves to the Existing Employees tab.

Admin Setup (Coming Soon) →

What's Next

  1. Review both forms — check every field, the conditional logic (P45/statement, TUPE, director, pay types), and the overall flow.
  2. Admin setup form — a simple page where your team enters an employer name and default email. This creates the Google Sheet and generates the unique employee form link.
  3. Google Apps Script backend — connects both forms to Google Sheets, sends email notifications, and runs the hourly check for new starters.
  4. CSV export & archive — one-click export to BrightPay-formatted CSV, with automatic move to the Existing Employees tab and timestamping.
